About

I'm Jami No and I am a UX/UI designer. My goals are to create meaningful designs and never stop learning

With a background in interior architecture and psychology, I have always had a propensity for analyzing user needs and strive to take a human centered approach. Having worked in a design industry, I am experienced with creating deliverables with the goals of stakeholders and the needs of users in mind. From designing the built environment experience to designing digital experiences, my core motivation has not changed. It is the belief that design can improve people's lives by solving every day problems with creative solutions.
