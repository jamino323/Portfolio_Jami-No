Overview

Shop Made in DC is a local retail initiative that sells products made by local makers. Their current website needs a design refresh they would like to add e-commerce capabilities. I designed a new website as well as creating a new e-commerce experience for their users.

    Competitve Analysis

    I performed a competitive analysis on Etsy because of their ecommerce platform and similarities in that they sell products made by individual makers. I found that Etsy has a unique place in the e-commerce market due to their unique product offerings and artisinal vibes. Although Etsy provides products made from individual makers, users can be innundated with too many choices and repetitive products. Shop Made in DC's would be unique in that their products represent a community and would offer more "bespoke" products.

    Interviews

    I perfomed 5 in-person interviews with customers at Shop Made in DC after their check out process. The purpose was to get a sense of customer’s interactions with Shop Made in DC and to gain insights into how customers might interact with Shop Made in DC’s e-commerce marketplace.
